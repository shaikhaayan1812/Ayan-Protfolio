Event Rules

01

Registration

All participants must register online before the event. On-spot registration is subject to availability.

02

Code of Conduct

Respect all participants, speakers, and staff. Harassment of any kind leads to immediate disqualification.

03

Hackathon Rules

Teams of 2-4 members. All code must be written during the event. Pre-written code is not allowed.

04

ID Cards

Carry your college/institute ID and event pass at all times. Entry without pass will be restricted.

05

Photography

Photography and videography are allowed. By attending, you consent to being featured in event media.

06

Refund Policy

Registration fees are non-refundable. Ticket transfers are allowed up to 5 days before the event.
