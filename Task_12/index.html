Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services do you offer?

We offer web development, mobile app development, cloud solutions, digital marketing, and IT consulting services tailored to your business needs.

2. How can I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button on top right, fill in your details, verify your email, and you're ready to go. It takes less than 2 minutes.

3. Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we use 256-bit SSL encryption and follow industry best practices to protect your data. We never share your information with third parties.

4. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay, and bank transfers. All transactions are securely processed.

5. How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page, click "Forgot Password", enter your registered email, and we'll send you a password reset link within minutes.

6. Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our plans. Contact support within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

7. How do I contact technical support?

You can reach us via live chat, email at support@faqportal.com, or call us at +1-800-555-0199. We're available 24/7.

8. Do you offer custom plans?

Absolutely! Contact our sales team with your requirements, and we'll create a custom plan that fits your budget and needs.

9. How long does it take to get started?

Basic setup takes about 5 minutes. For complex enterprise solutions, our team typically completes onboarding within 48 hours.

10. Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the billing accordingly.

11. What is your uptime guarantee?

We guarantee 99.9% uptime for all our services. If we fall below that, you'll receive service credits as compensation.

12. Do you have a mobile app?

Yes, our mobile app is available on both iOS and Android. You can manage your account, track orders, and contact support on the go.

13. How do I delete my account?

Go to Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Confirm your decision, and your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

14. Do you offer discounts for startups?

Yes, we have special pricing for startups and non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team to learn more about our discount programs.

15. What happens after my free trial ends?

Your account will automatically switch to the free plan with limited features. Upgrade to a paid plan anytime to unlock full access.
