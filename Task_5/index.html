★ 4.9 · Tropical Paradise

Maldives

Crystal-clear lagoons, powdery white beaches, and overwater villas — the ultimate island escape

About Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise of 1,190 coral islands grouped across 26 atolls in the Indian Ocean. Known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, pristine white-sand beaches, and luxurious overwater villas, it's the world's premier destination for romance, relaxation, and water sports.

Trip Highlights

🏖️

Private Beaches

Secluded white sand beaches with crystal clear waters

🏠

Overwater Villas

Luxury villas with glass floors and private pools

🐠

Coral Reefs

World-class snorkeling and diving spots

🌅

Sunset Cruises

Traditional dhoni boat rides at golden hour

🍽️

Underwater Dining

Restaurants surrounded by marine life

💆

Beach Spa

Overwater spa treatments with ocean views

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Malé

Welcome at Velana International Airport. Speedboat transfer to your resort. Welcome drink and sunset orientation.

Day 2

Beach & Water Sports

Morning snorkeling in the house reef. Jet skiing and parasailing. Beachside barbecue dinner.

Day 3

Island Hopping

Visit local fishing island. Sandbank picnic. Dolphin cruise at sunset. Underwater restaurant dinner.

Day 4

Spa & Relaxation

Full-day spa package. Overwater villa relaxation. Private beach candlelight dinner.

Day 5

Departure

Final breakfast over the lagoon. Souvenir shopping. Speedboat transfer to airport.

★ 4.8 · Island of Gods

Bali

Rice terraces, ancient temples, vibrant culture, and tropical bliss — Bali is a feast for the soul

About Bali

Bali, the "Island of the Gods," is a world-renowned destination known for its lush rice terraces, ancient Hindu temples, vibrant arts scene, and stunning beaches. From the cultural heart of Ubud to the surf breaks of Seminyak, Bali offers a rich and diverse travel experience.

Trip Highlights

🛕

Uluwatu Temple

Clifftop temple with stunning ocean views

🌾

Tegalalang Rice Terraces

Iconic green terraces and swinging spots

🐒

Monkey Forest

Sacred forest with playful monkeys

🌋

Mount Batur

Sunrise trek to an active volcano

🏄

Surfing

World-class waves at Uluwatu & Padang Padang

💆

Bali Spa

Traditional massages and wellness retreats

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Bali

Airport pickup. Check into resort in Seminyak. Evening beach walk and welcome dinner.

Day 2

Ubud Cultural Tour

Visit Tegallalang Rice Terraces, Monkey Forest, Ubud Palace. Traditional Balinese dance performance.

Day 3

Uluwatu & Beaches

Uluwatu Temple visit. Surfing at Padang Padang. Kecak fire dance at sunset.

Day 4

Adventure Day

Mount Batur sunrise trek. White water rafting in Ayung River. Spa evening.

Day 5

Departure

Leisurely breakfast. Last-minute shopping at local markets. Transfer to Ngurah Rai Airport.

Switzerland

Towering Alps, pristine lakes, charming villages — experience the magic of Switzerland

About Switzerland

Switzerland is the epitome of natural beauty — from the majestic Matterhorn to the serene shores of Lake Geneva. Known for its precision, luxury, and breathtaking landscapes, it's a dream destination for nature lovers, skiers, and romantics alike.

Trip Highlights

🏔️

Matterhorn

Iconic Alpine peak in Zermatt

🚂

Glacier Express

Scenic train ride through the Alps

🏞️

Lake Geneva

Crystal clear lake with Alpine views

🏘️

Interlaken

Adventure capital between two lakes

🎿

Skiing

World-class slopes in St. Moritz

🍫

Chocolate Tour

Visit Swiss chocolate factories

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Zurich

Arrive at Zurich Airport. Transfer to hotel. Old Town walking tour. Dinner by the lake.

Day 2

Zurich to Interlaken

Train ride through scenic countryside. Arrive in Interlaken. Paragliding or lake cruise.

Day 3

Jungfraujoch Excursion

Top of Europe — train to Jungfraujoch. Ice palace, panoramic views, snow fun.

Day 4

Lucerne & Chapel Bridge

Visit Chapel Bridge, Lion Monument. Boat ride on Lake Lucerne. Swiss fondue dinner.

Day 5

Departure

Breakfast with Alpine views. Chocolate shopping. Transfer to Zurich Airport.

Goa

Golden beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and tropical vibes — Goa is India's party capital

Goa is India's smallest but most vibrant state, famous for its stunning beaches, Portuguese colonial architecture, and laid-back hippie culture. From the bustling shores of Baga to the serene sands of Palolem, Goa offers sun, surf, seafood, and soul.

🏖️

⛪

Serene crescent-shaped beach paradise

🍹

Nightlife

Beach parties, clubs, and live music

🛕

Fort Aguada

17th-century Portuguese fort with ocean views

🐊

Dudhsagar Falls

Majestic waterfall and jungle trek

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Goa

Airport pickup. Check into beach resort. Evening walk on Baga Beach. Seafood dinner.

Day 2

North Goa Tour

Visit Fort Aguada, Calangute Beach, Anjuna Flea Market. Sunset at Chapora Fort.

Day 3

South Goa Serenity

Palolem Beach relaxation. Dolphin spotting boat ride. Portuguese colonial walk.

Day 4

Adventure & Waterfalls

Dudhsagar Waterfall trek. River rafting. Casino cruise in the evening.

Day 5

Departure

Beachside breakfast. Spice plantation tour. Transfer to Goa International Airport.
